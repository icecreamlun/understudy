# Internal Procurement Policy

Vendors that process customer data must provide current SOC 2 Type II evidence, breach notification within 72 hours, data deletion within 30 days of termination, and a named security contact.

Contracts must not auto-renew for longer than 12 months without written approval from Finance.

Any vendor using subcontractors must provide a subprocessors list and notify the company before material changes.

Payment terms should be net 30 or better unless Finance approves an exception.
